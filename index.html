Our services range from wellness care, infertility, American drugs, obstetrics, gynecology, urology, cardiology and nephrology. We also have a foreign transfer patients program, where we treat patients with other complicated health conditions and couples desirous of giving birth in the United States of America for higher level of care.

We maintain affiliations and/or referral relationships with facilities in the United States of America. Griggs Specialist Hospital, Lagos is affiliated with the Houston Texas, Obstetrics and Gynecology (OB/GYN) clinic of Kanayo E. Ubesie MD, PA., a US-based S Corporation, registered in the State of Texas, USA. The practice established a Foreign Transfer Patient Program, which accepts transfer patients from all over the world, who seek US level of health care.

Cancer Diagnosis

Patients are referred to The University of Texas, MD Anderson Cancer Center, Houston, Texas USA. The University of Texas, MD Anderson Cancer Center is one of the original three comprehensive cancer centers in the United States of America, established by the US National Cancer Act of 1971.

The center and its multiple affiliates still maintain the top position in the world, with regards to cancer diagnosis, treatment, technology and innovation. Please visit the MD Anderson Cancer Center website.
