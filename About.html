About Us

GRIGGS SPECIALIST HOSPITAL offers a diverse range of medical and surgical specialties. The hospital is strongly linked to specialists and hospitals in USA through their consultants and practitioners that are currently practicing in the USA. These physicians and their Nigerian counterparts provide a technology based, unique, modern, standard and innovative mode of medical care equal to any in other parts of the world.
